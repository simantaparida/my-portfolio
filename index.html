how i work

I think like a product manager & work like a designer.

Being from an entrepreneurial background, I always focus on user needs without neglecting the business requirements.

I strongly believe 80% of a problem is solved when we dig deeper & deeper into that problem. Rest 20% is solved when we streamline all the research findings in a well-planned design process.

I typically follow an end-to-end design process that starts with problem statement, and business goals continues with user research, wireframing, UI design, prototyping, usability testing and shipping for the development with appropriate documentation.
