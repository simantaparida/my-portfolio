Luxury Bedding Brand Store for HNIs

Client: Malabar Hill Home

Project: Crafted a premium bedding online store for the rich (B2C)

Project Overview:

  • Project: Premium Online Store
  • Client: Malabar Hill Home
  • Industry: Retail
  • Services: UX Design, Visual Design
  • Tools: Sketch, Figma
  • Timeline: 3 Months

Project Summary

Malabar Hill Home is a luxury bedding brand catering to high-net-worth individuals. The objective of this project was to create a premium online store that reflects the brand’s luxury image and provides a seamless shopping experience for its exclusive clientele.

The website features personalized product recommendations, high-quality images, detailed product descriptions, and a smooth checkout process.

The project was completed on time, exceeding client expectations in terms of design aesthetics and user engagement.

Problem Statement

The existing website lacked the visual appeal and user-friendly interface required for a luxury brand. Navigation was cumbersome, and the checkout process was not optimized, leading to a high cart abandonment rate.

Business Requirements & Goals

The primary business goal was to enhance the brand's online presence and boost sales through an improved user experience.

Key requirements included:

  • High-quality product images
  • Personalized product recommendations
  • Detailed product descriptions
  • Intuitive navigation
  • Streamlined checkout process

Requirement Gathering

I conducted interviews with stakeholders and performed competitive analysis to understand the needs and preferences of the target audience.

Key findings included:

  • High-quality imagery and detailed product information are essential
  • A quick and smooth checkout process is crucial
  • Personalized recommendations enhance the shopping experience

Summary Research

The research revealed that the target audience values a seamless shopping experience and high-quality products. Competitors lacked a refined online presence, providing an opportunity to stand out.

Competitive Analysis

The analysis compared the website features of top competitors and identified key areas for improvement. The findings were used to create a feature list to give the client a competitive edge.

User Persona

To help me think from the user’s perspective, I created 2 fictional personas featuring 2 prime user types based on the input from the client and ran them through the Product Manager to get the initial feedback.

I wanted to make sure that the people using my designs had the best experience possible, so I came up with 2 user personas. This helped me get to know my users better and understand what they wanted and needed from the website.

Prototypes

Created interactive prototypes to test user flow and functionality. Used usability testing feedback to make iterative improvements.

Learnings

The project highlighted the importance of a user-centered design approach. Continuous user feedback and iterative design improvements are key to achieving the best results.
